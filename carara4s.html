The 2020 Porsche 911 Carrera 4S is a German sports car manufactured by Porsche. It is part of the eighth generation of the Porsche 911 known as the Porsche 992. Under the hood, the 911 Carrera 4S comes equipped with a 3.0L flat-six engine capable of developing 443 horsepower at 6,500 revolutions per minute (RPM). THE CREW 2 The Porsche 911 Carrera 4S made its debut in The Crew 2 as part of the Summer in Hollywood update on July 8, 2020, alongside with the Koenigsegg One:1, where it is listed a TC2LogoSR Street Race and is only available as such. Players can purchase the vehicle with either Bucks 613,900 or with Crew Credits 87,700. It is the first time ever that the 2020 Porsche 911 Carrera 4S makes an appearance in a video game on any platform (including mobile).
