The Porsche Panamera is a five—door sports fastback of the Gran Turismo class with a front-engine layout and all-wheel or rear-wheel drive. Production and sales of the car began in 2009. The car was named Panamera due to the successful performances of Porsche in the Carrera Panamericana race, held in the 50s of the XX century. As of September 2010, over 25,000 vehicles have been sold. In the summer of 2013, the Panamera underwent an upgrade, the running and rear lights were refreshed, and improvements appeared in the cabin.
