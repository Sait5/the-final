The Porsche Taycan (internal designation Porsche Mission E) is an all-electric 4-door sedan developed by Porsche. The concept car was first shown at the Frankfurt International Motor Show in 2015. This is the first full-fledged Porsche electric car, with the exception of historical models like the Porsche P1 and Lohner-Porsche, designed by Ferdinand Porsche. According to the statement of Porsche Chairman Oliver Blume to CAR Magazine, made in September 2017, the Mission E project cars will "cost like the entry-level Panamera", the price of which starts from 85,000 US dollars. At the same time, the publication "Autorevue" makes an estimate of the cost of about 200 thousand euros. The electric car is planned to be sold in several variants with different characteristics similar to other Porsche models.
